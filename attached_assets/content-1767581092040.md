It weeds out mismatches early and attracts serious inquiries. Most successful freelance/small agency sites do this effectively with clear, simple layouts.

#### Recommended Structure: Create a Dedicated Pricing Section/Page

- Add it to your existing #services section or make a new anchor link like #pricing.
- Use **"Starting at"** phrasing for flexibility (e.g., "Starting at $599") since projects can vary. This avoids locking you in while still being upfront.
- Frame everything positively: Emphasize affordability for local SMBs, value (e.g., "Professional sites that grow your business without breaking the bank"), and what's included.
